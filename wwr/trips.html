Trips Available

Beginner

This adventure is designed for those with little to none experienced. It's purpose is to help those looking for something new dip their toes into the world of White Water Rafting. This is a shorter course with mild rapids that is also great for groups looking to do this as a family who may have younger participants.

Intermediate

Our intermediate course is for someone who wants to challenge themselves and gain a better grasp on the art of White Water Rafting. Dry Oar Rafting Co. saves this trip for someone who wants to get their adrenaline pumping but still remain completely confident in their ability to navigate the rapids, drops, and obstacles that mother nature throws at them.

Advanced

The advanced course is one for the books, designed for those looking to be surprised with obstacles around each corner. This course with challenge your experience and abilities to the max as you navigate the intense rapids and constant splashing of water while maintaining impressive teamwork to traverse the unforseen. This is not recommended for groups with children or those that are unexperienced.
